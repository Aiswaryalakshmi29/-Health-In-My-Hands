## The Problem Statement
### Healthcare facilities, including hospitals and clinics, often face challenges in managing patient information, appointments, and treatment plans efficiently. This can lead to long waiting times, errors in patient records, and overall dissatisfaction for both patients and healthcare providers.
## The Solution
### The Efficient Patient Management System will improve the overall patient experience, reduce administrative workload, and enhance the quality of healthcare services. It will provide healthcare providers with a reliable tool to manage patient data effectively and improve operational efficiency.
